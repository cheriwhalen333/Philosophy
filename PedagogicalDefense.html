Term Definition

¬π GEM Definition: A Generative AI Educational Model (GEM) is a private, agentic Google AI tool. Unlike public AI interfaces, a GEM is a secure, sandboxed environment where data remains confidential to the creator and is not used to train public models. It functions as a specialized digital assistant tailored to specific instructional datasets and pedagogical goals.
